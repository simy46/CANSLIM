The Origin of CAN SLIM

The CAN SLIM strategy was developed by William J. O'Neil, the founder of Investor's Business Daily. It is a powerful method for identifying growth stocks using a combination of fundamental and technical analysis. Each letter in the acronym CAN SLIM stands for a key criterion:

  • C: Current quarterly earnings per share (EPS) increase
  • A: Annual earnings increases
  • N: New products, management, or other new events
  • S: Supply and demand (shares outstanding)
  • L: Leader or laggard
  • I: Institutional sponsorship
  • M: Market direction
