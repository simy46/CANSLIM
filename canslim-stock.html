Options Details

An option is a financial contract that gives the buyer the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset at a predetermined price before or on a specific date.

Types of Options

  • Call Options: The right to buy the underlying asset.
  • Put Options: The right to sell the underlying asset.
